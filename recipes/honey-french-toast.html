Honey French Toast

Description

This French Toast recipe is really simple, healthy, and tastes so good you will never get bored of it

Ingredients

  • Toast Bread
  • Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tbsp Honey

Steps

  1. Add some butter on a pan
  2. Crack the egg and stir it in a bowl
  3. Dip the toast in the egg bowl
  4. Place the toast on a pan
  5. Let it cook for a few minutes
  6. Remove the toast and add the honey
  7. Bon appetit
