Banana Oats Meal

Description

This Banana Oats Meal is a really healthy plate that you can make anytime, makes you feel full, and all the ingredients are pretty easy.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Oats
  • Honey
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Add 100g of Oats to a bowl
  2. Add 1 tbsp of honey
  3. Heat up the Milk
  4. Pour the Milk on the oats
  5. Let the oats in the milk for 3 minutes
  6. Add your banana
  7. Bon appetit
